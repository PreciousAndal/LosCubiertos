    Los Cubiertos

    WHO WE ARE.

    At Los Cubiertos, we're more than just a restaurant - we're a family. We believe that food brings people together and creates memories that last a lifetime. Our team is made up of passionate chefs, servers, and bartenders who are dedicated to providing exceptional service and creating a warm and welcoming atmosphere for our customers.

    WHAT WE'RE ABOUT.

    We're all about celebrating the flavors of the Philippines and sharing them with the world. Our menu features a variety of traditional dishes, including sisig, dinakdakan, and lechon, as well as our signature Boodle Fight Bundles that are perfect for sharing with family and friends. And let's not forget our famous desserts, like the refreshing halo-halo and the decadent combination of leche flan and ube.

    WHERE WE COME FROM.

    We're proud to be a part of the vibrant community of Pagsanjan, Laguna. Our ingredients are sourced from local suppliers, and our recipes are inspired by the rich culinary traditions of the Philippines. We're committed to supporting our community and giving back through events and partnerships with local organizations.
